From Vision to Reality

Founded in 2010, Plysource began with a clear vision: to revolutionize Kerala's interior materials industry by providing premium quality PVC, WPC, and plywood solutions.

What started as a modest operation has blossomed into Kerala's most trusted supplier, serving contractors, builders, and homeowners across all 14 districts.

Our Mission

To provide premium quality interior materials that empower our customers to bring their construction visions to life, while maintaining the highest standards of service and reliability.

Our Journey

2010

Plysource was founded with a vision to provide quality interior materials to Kerala's growing construction sector.

2012

Expanded our product range to include PVC boards, responding to market demand for water-resistant materials.

2015

Introduced WPC boards to our catalog, offering eco-friendly alternatives to traditional wood products.

2018

Launched statewide delivery service, covering all 14 districts of Kerala with reliable logistics.

2023

Celebrated 15 years of serving Kerala's construction industry with 2000+ satisfied customers.
